Quality of Life

  • Stacks: Use/ETC stack up to 9,999 per slot.
  • No Breath: CC / enter FM immediately after combat.
  • Persistence: Buffs persist on DC; expedition recall windows & reconnect grace after clear.
  • Input & Chat: PIC is typable; chat censorship removed.
  • Fishing: @fish at FM (Lv150); exchange via Obrin.
  • Movement: Attack while moving; Double Jump enabled (press F12 to toggle OFF for flying mobs).

Merit System (Lv 200+)

  • Post-200 EXP converts to Merit EXP.
  • Requirement: Next Merit level needs (next level × 100,000,000) EXP.
  • Cap: Merit Level 1,000.
  • Reward: +1 Ability Point per Merit Level.
  • Track with @merit.

Expeditions (overview)

  • Entry Limits:
    • Daily 1×: Zakum, Krexel, Scarga, Horntail.
    • Weekly 1×: The Boss, Pink Bean, Chaos Zakum, Chaos Horntail, Von Leon, Empress.
  • Reconnection grace: 5 minutes if all members disconnect.
  • Post-clear grace: Expedition ends 2 minutes after CLEAR; disconnected players can rejoin to loot within the window.
Tip: Use the in-game updater.exe whenever possible; only grab full clients when explicitly required.
