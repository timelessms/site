  • 25 × Chaos Scroll → Godly Chaos Scroll (GCS) — 50% chance, +3 to +7 stats.

Market‑Driven Pricing (via Loki)

Market‑Driven Chaos Scroll Exchange, through Loki at the Free Market!

How it works

  • Buyers: Each scroll you purchase increases the next scroll’s price by 1%, compounding with each unit bought.
  • Sellers: Each scroll you sell decreases the next scroll’s price by 1%, compounding with each unit sold.
  • Bulk transactions: Buying or selling multiple scrolls applies these 1% adjustments sequentially — later units cost progressively more (when buying) or yield progressively less (when selling). The final adjusted price becomes the new base for everyone.

Using the Exchange

  1. Visit Loki in the Free Market.
  2. Select Chaos Scroll Exchange (Market Price) from the menu.
  3. Choose Buy or Sell, enter your quantity, and confirm.

Watch the market shift in real time as players trade, and plan your purchases or sales to grab the best deal before the next price change!

End-Game Gear

  • Timeless Gear — Crafted from Rock of Time dropped by Pink Bean.
  • Von Leon Gear — Exchanged using Von Leon's Seal dropped by Von Leon.
  • Empress Gear — Exchanged using Cygnus Circlet.
  • Leveling: Von Leon Gear uses Cosmic Fragments (drop from mobs in Lionheart Castle, LHC).
  • Leveling: Empress Gear uses Greater Cosmic Fragments (drop from mobs in Twilight Perion).

Fusion: Von Leon Gear can be fused into Empress Gear, transferring 20% of its stats. The Von Leon piece used is consumed.
